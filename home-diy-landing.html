The real challenge

Your CRM is full of data.
Your results don't show it.

Home & DIY retail has six structural problems that prevent CRM from delivering results — and most teams don't know which one is bleeding them most.

70%+

Research online. Buy in-store. You see nothing.

The majority of furniture decisions start online but close at POS — with zero attribution. Your best customer walked through your door last week. You have no idea they were browsing your e-shop for three months first.

We cover this in the webinar
3–5 yrs

Low frequency. One wrong moment = one lost customer.

The average Home & DIY customer buys major items every 3–5 years. Miss the trigger — a move, a renovation, a new baby — and your competitor wins the lifetime value. Without predictive segmentation, you're always guessing.

AI trigger models — covered May 8
40–60%

Your database is dead weight — and you're paying for it.

Forensic audits consistently show that 40–60% of retail databases contain duplicates, expired consents, POS typos, or unreachable contacts. Every email sent to these burns deliverability — and real revenue.

Q1–Q2

Move-in season is 60% of your annual CRM opportunity.

Spring brings movers, renovators, and new-home buyers — all hunting for furniture at once. Without automated nurture flows triggered by seasonal signals, you're relying on manual campaigns during the busiest 8 weeks of the year.
